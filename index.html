About

I’m someone who finds stories in visuals, in color, movement, and silence. My work lives between video editing, design, and brand storytelling, where every frame tells its own story. I’ve been part of projects that shaped how people see and feel a brand, but what fulfils me most is the craft itself — turning raw ideas into something that connects. I enjoy creating visuals that convey simple yet lasting messages. Whether it’s building a brand’s visual rhythm or cutting a scene that feels honest, my goal is always the same: to make things that feel real, human, and alive.
