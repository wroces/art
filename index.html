About

I’m someone who finds stories in visuals — in color, movement, and silence. My work lives between video editing, design, and brand storytelling, where every frame or layout has a reason to exist. I’ve been part of projects that shaped how people see and feel a brand, but what fulfills me most is the craft itself — turning raw ideas into something that connects. I like creating visuals that speak simply but stay with you. Whether it’s building a brand’s visual rhythm or cutting a scene that feels honest, my goal is always the same: to make things that feel real, human, and alive.
